BMS Hostel

The B.M.S. Educational Trust (BMSET) is a prestigious institution which founded and manages the top class colleges viz., B.M.S. College of Engineering (BMSCE) , BMS Institute of Technology & Management, BMS College of Law, BMS College for women and others institutions.
B.M.S. Educational Trust Hostels are part of BMSET and BMSET Hostels in the campus of B.M.S. College of Engineering (BMSCE) provide accommodation to the students wishing to reside in the hostels. BMSET Hostels gives a distinct advantage of befriending with students from across the globe and expose them different religions, languages and customs of countries. Additionally, living as a community in the hostel will lead to all-round development of personality of a hostelite and will definitely go a long way in fostering world-class citizens.
